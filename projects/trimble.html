Trimble - Hardware Installation and Management

Hardware Installation and Management

Android & Web New Feature Design

Trimble Unity Remote Monitoring software, combined with its hardware of IoT wireless data recorders, helps water utility companies proactively monitor asset performance, optimize water operations, reduce asset failure and repair costs, and gather critical telemetry water data for reporting.

This is my first project as a sole UX designer for a brand new feature while working remotely.

PROCESS & COLLABORATIONS

Solve complex and ambiguous problems by collabration!

There are two major personal challenges for me starting this project:
1. I am working remotely;
2. This project requires a lot of domain languages in smart water management.

Therefore, throughout the design process, I organized a lot of cross-team meetings to initiate discussions, consolidate information, and gather feedbacks for prototypes at different stages. These collaborations allowed me to solve ambiguous and complex problems with the inputs of my coworkers.

With Senior UX Designer, Front-end Engineers

The UX team at TrimbleWater is a small team which is still exploring an efficient, effective process to deliver a useful and consistent product. In the spam of this project, I crafted the first draft of Trimble Unity style guide for both web and mobile with the hope of increasing team efficiency and product consistency. The style guide is available in sketch and InVision DSM.

I also worked with iOS and Android engineer to unify UI components across these two platforms, given the chance of implementing new UIs that affect the whole app globally.
